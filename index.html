Mechanics of Materials and Structures

Advanced Materials in Structural Engineering and Design research group explores the use of cutting-edge materials in structural engineering. Through a multidisciplinary approach, the group conducts experimental investigations and computational simulations to understand the behavior and optimize the performance of structures incorporating advanced materials. Collaborating with industry partners and research institutions, our work aims to revolutionize structural design, improve sustainability, and enhance resilience. Our research contributes to creating safer and more innovative structures for the future.
